About Me

I'm Maria Joseph (aka mjosewings on GitHub), a coding enthusiast with a flair for style, sarcasm, and social impact. I'm a proud member of the Black, LGBTQ+, and autistic communities. I created VitaSilens to highlight not only my coding projects but also voices often overlooked in tech.

From bioinformatics to website building, I’m constantly learning and teaching others as I go. This site is a tribute to that journey—with love, coffee, and lots of error messages.

Personal Background

I grew up balancing a love for science, technology, and creativity. With a deep passion for making information accessible and beautiful, I found myself drawn to the intersections of biology, data, and design. Every project I take on is a blend of logic and artistry—where code meets storytelling.

My Journey

My path hasn’t always been linear. It started with a fascination for the hidden patterns in life, leading me to bioinformatics and software development. Along the way, I faced the challenges of navigating spaces that weren't always made for people like me—but I stayed grounded, driven, and a little bit defiant. Today, VitaSilens stands as a reflection of resilience, creativity, and the belief that every story deserves to be heard—even the quiet ones.
