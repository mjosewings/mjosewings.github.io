About Me

I'm MJ (aka mjosewings on GitHub), a coding enthusiast with a flair for style, sarcasm, and social impact. I'm a proud member of the Black, LGBTQ+, and autistic communities. I created SilentVitas to highlight not only my coding projects but also voices often overlooked in tech.

From bioinformatics to website building, I’m constantly learning and teaching others as I go. This site is a tribute to that journey—with love, coffee, and lots of error messages.
